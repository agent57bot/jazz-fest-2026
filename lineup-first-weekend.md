## Planning Notes

- Gates open: 11:00 AM daily
- Music starts: 11:30 AM
- Festival ends: 7:00 PM
- Multiple stages run simultaneously - conflicts are inevitable
- Rain or shine event
- Bring: sun protection, comfortable shoes, cash for vendors
- Festival food highlights: Crawfish Monica, Cochon de Lait po-boys, Mango Freeze
