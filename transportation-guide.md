# Transportation Guide - Fair Grounds to Quarter House

## Your Situation
- **From:** Fair Grounds Race Course (1751 Gentilly Blvd)
- **To:** Quarter House (129 Chartres St, French Quarter)
- **Distance:** ~2.5 miles
- **Your style:** Stay through closing (7 PM), walk a few blocks before catching ride
- **Challenge:** Avoiding post-Jazz Fest traffic chaos

## The Traffic Reality

**What happens at 7 PM closing:**
- 50,000+ people leaving at once
- Esplanade Ave (main route) becomes parking lot
- Rideshare surge pricing kicks in immediately
- Rideshare pickup zones are mobbed
- Traffic doesn't clear until ~8:30 PM

**Your walk-away strategy is SMART** - you're already ahead of most people.

## Best Transportation Options (Ranked)

### Option 1: Walk + Strategic Rideshare Pickup (RECOMMENDED)

**The Strategy:**
1. Leave fairgrounds at 7 PM
2. Walk **north** on Gentilly Blvd away from Esplanade (opposite of traffic flow)
3. Walk 0.5 - 0.7 miles (~10-15 min) to **City Park area** (Wisner Blvd or Orleans Ave)
4. Request rideshare from there (~7:15-7:20 PM)

**Why this works:**
- You're moving AWAY from traffic bottleneck
- City Park area has street parking, easier pickup
- By 7:20 PM, surge has started dropping
- Driver can approach from north, avoiding Esplanade gridlock
- ~10 min ride to Quarter House from there

**Pickup zones to target:**
- Wisner Blvd near City Park
- Orleans Ave & N Carrollton Ave (Parkway Tavern po-boy shop nearby - grab food while you wait)
- Morning Call Coffee Stand parking lot (in City Park) - **IDEAL**: well-lit, safe, easy pickup

### Option 2: Walk to Bayou St. John + Rideshare

**The Strategy:**
1. Exit Fair Grounds, walk northwest toward Bayou St. John neighborhood
2. Head to **Esplanade Ave & Moss St** area (0.6 miles, ~12-15 min walk)
3. This is a residential neighborhood with restaurants/bars
4. Request rideshare from there

**Why this works:**
- Bayou St. John is charming, safe neighborhood
- Lots of porches/bars to wait at (1000 Figs, Toups Meatery, Cafe Degas nearby)
- Drivers can access via Bayou Road (avoids Esplanade)
- You're ~2 miles from Quarter House (short ride)

**Bonus:** If rideshare is still surging, grab a drink/snack and wait 20-30 min

### Option 3: Walk to Streetcar + Free Ride

**The Strategy:**
1. Walk to **Canal St & N. Carrollton Ave** (0.8 miles, ~15-20 min walk northeast)
2. Catch the **Canal Streetcar** (runs until midnight)
3. Ride to **Canal & Bourbon St** stop
4. Walk 3 blocks to Quarter House (129 Chartres)

**Cost:** $1.25 per person (cash or RTA card)

**Why this works:**
- Completely avoids traffic
- Predictable, no surge pricing
- Streetcar runs every 15-20 min
- Total time: ~45 min door-to-door
- You see the city, not stuck in traffic

**Downside:** Longer total time, requires walking to Canal St

### Option 4: Pre-Arranged Private Car (Group Coordination)

**For Laura to organize:**
- Book a **private car/van service** for 7:15 PM pickup
- Examples: NOLA Rides, Royal Rides, or local car service (NOT Uber/Lyft)
- Pre-arranged pickup at **Gentilly & Mystery St** (0.3 mi from Fair Grounds)
- Driver waits for you, no surge pricing
- Can split cost across group

**Cost:** ~$60-80 for private sedan, $100-120 for van (split among group)

**Why this works:**
- Guaranteed pickup (no waiting/searching)
- Driver knows to avoid Esplanade
- Can coordinate timing with group dinner reservations
- Professional drivers know back routes

### Option 5: Bike Share + Bike to French Quarter

**The Strategy:**
1. Grab **Blue Bikes** (NOLA bike share) near Fair Grounds
2. Bike to French Quarter via **quieter residential streets**
3. Dock bikes in French Quarter

**Route:** Gentilly → Bayou St. John → Esplanade Ridge → Rampart St → French Quarter
**Time:** ~20-25 min bike ride
**Cost:** $8 day pass per person

**Why this works:**
- Completely avoid traffic
- Fastest option if you're comfortable biking
- Bikes available at Fair Grounds and Quarter House area
- Scenic route through historic neighborhoods

**Downside:** Need to feel comfortable biking ~2.5 miles after a full day on your feet

## Worst Options (Avoid)

❌ **Rideshare from Fair Grounds at 7:00-7:15 PM**
- 3x-4x surge pricing
- 20-45 min wait for pickup
- Driver stuck in Esplanade traffic
- What should be a 15 min ride takes 45+ min

❌ **Driving your own car parked near Fair Grounds**
- Getting out of Gentilly takes 45+ min
- Every route to French Quarter is jammed
- Parking in French Quarter is expensive/difficult

## Pro Tips

1. **Download offline maps** - Cell service can be spotty with 50K people
2. **Cash for streetcar** - Have exact change ($1.25)
3. **Charge phones** - Battery pack recommended for rideshare/navigation
4. **Comfortable walking shoes** - You'll walk 5+ miles during the day, then more to escape traffic
5. **Headlamp or phone flashlight** - Some residential streets have poor lighting

## Weather Contingency

- Morning Call (Option 1) has covered outdoor area
- Toups Meatery (Option 2) can call you a rideshare from inside
- Streetcar (Option 3) is covered
