## Neighborhood Info

### Garden District / Irish Channel
- Quiet residential area with beautiful historic homes
- St. Charles streetcar provides easy transit
- Safe, walkable neighborhood
- Good restaurant scene on Magazine Street and St. Charles Ave

### French Quarter
- Heart of tourist activity
- Walking distance to most French Quarter attractions
- Louder, more crowded, especially evenings/weekends
- Closer to some music venues on Frenchmen Street

## Getting Around

- **St. Charles Streetcar:** $1.25/ride, connects Garden District to downtown
- **Rideshare:** Uber/Lyft readily available
- **Walking:** Garden District is very walkable
- **Car:** Parking can be challenging, especially in French Quarter
