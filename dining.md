# Dining & Food

## Confirmed Restaurants

### Superior Seafood & Oyster Bar
- **Address:** 4338 St Charles Ave
- **Style:** Cajun/Creole seafood
- **Highlights:** Fresh oysters, seafood platters
- **Reservation:** Recommended for dinner
- **Notes:** On St. Charles streetcar line, easy access from Constantinople St

## Recommendations to Explore

### French Quarter

- **Café Du Monde:** Beignets & café au lait (no reservations, expect lines)
- **Galatoire's:** Classic Creole fine dining
- **Napoleon House:** Muffuletta sandwiches, historic atmosphere
- **Central Grocery:** Original muffuletta
- **Acme Oyster House:** Raw oysters, casual atmosphere

### Garden District / Uptown

- **Commander's Palace:** Legendary Creole restaurant (jackets suggested)
- **Emeril's Delmonico:** Fine dining, Emeril Lagasse restaurant
- **Casamento's:** Oyster house (seasonal, closes in summer)
- **Parkway Bakery & Tavern:** Po-boys

### Magazine Street

- **Stein's Market & Deli:** Sandwiches
- **La Petite Grocery:** Contemporary bistro
- **Dat Dog:** Gourmet hot dogs

### Festival Food

Jazz Fest is famous for festival food:
- Crawfish Monica
- Cochon de Lait po-boys
- Mango Freeze
- Crawfish bread
- Soft shell crab po-boys
