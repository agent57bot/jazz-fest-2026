# Grand Marshal Pass Guide - Jazz Fest 2026

## What You Get

**Grand Marshal (GM) pass = Premium standing-room access at the three main stages:**
- Festival Stage (biggest headliners)
- Shell Gentilly Stage
- Congo Square Stage

**PLUS:**
- Private GM Hospitality Tent (complimentary snacks, private bar, restrooms, shade, fans)
- Front-and-center viewing space
- Re-entry privileges
- Dedicated VIP entrance gates

## Critical Entry Information

### Your Pass Components

**You need BOTH to access GM areas:**
1. **Laminated Grand Marshal pass** (your ticket)
2. **Daily wristband** (issued at gate each day)

**IMPORTANT:**
- Laminated pass has **daily tabs on the corner** — DO NOT remove them yourself
- Gate staff removes the tab and gives you your daily wristband
- Without the tab, you can't get your wristband
- Without the wristband, you can't access GM areas
- Ripped/broken wristbands = no entry (wear it securely)

### Where to Get Your Wristband

**Pedestrians (most people):**
- Gentilly St. gate OR Sauvage St. gate
- Look for **"VIP Ticket Entrance"** signs
- **DO NOT LEAVE THE GATE AREA** until you have your wristband

**If using VIP shuttle:**
- Upon exiting shuttle

**If you have VIP parking pass:**
- Trafalgar St. vehicular gate

### Children & Wristbands

- **2 years and older:** Need both pass AND wristband
- **Under 2 years:** Need wristband only (request from gate staff), no pass required

## Accessing GM Stage Areas

### How to Enter GM Sections

**All three GM stage areas:**
- Entry is from **stage right** (the LEFT side of the stage when you're facing it)
- Look for **"GM"** signs marking the entrance
- Show both your laminated pass AND wristband

**At gate, you'll receive:**
- Map showing all GM areas
- Daily music schedule

## GM Hospitality Tent

### Location
- Behind the **Food One** area
- On the grass between **Economy Hall Tent** and **Jazz & Heritage Stage**
- NOT accessible from the dirt track
- Consult your map (given at entry)

### What's Inside
- ✅ **Complimentary light local snacks**
- ✅ **Private beverage stand** (full bar: beer, wine, spirits, water) - for purchase
- ✅ **Shade & fans**
- ✅ **Tables & chairs**
- ✅ **Private restrooms**

**Payment:** Cashless only (credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay)

### Restrooms
- Private restrooms in GM Hospitality Tent
- Portable toilets adjacent to Festival Stage GM area
- Portable toilets adjacent to Shell Gentilly Stage GM area

## Pass Rules & Transferability

### Daily Transferability
- **GM pass IS transferable from day to day**
- **NOT transferable within a single day**
- Whoever gets the wristband that morning owns the pass for the entire day
- Once you've accepted your wristband, you have full benefits for that day

**Example:**
- ✅ OK: Matt uses pass Thursday, Dawn uses same pass Friday
- ❌ NOT OK: Matt gets wristband Thursday morning, gives pass to Dawn Thursday afternoon

### Re-Entry Privileges

**You CAN re-enter:**
- Leave and come back through pedestrian gates (Gentilly or Sauvage)
- Must show laminated pass + wristband

**Cars CANNOT re-enter:**
- If you drive out of parking area, you're done for the day
- Walk in/out if you need to leave temporarily

### Access Limitations

**GM pass does NOT include:**
- Big Chief area (separate pass needed)
- Krewe of Jazz Fest area (separate pass needed)
- GA+ areas (separate pass needed)

## Pro Tips for GM Pass Holders

### Maximize Your Value

**Front-stage strategy:**
1. Arrive 30-45 min before your must-see act
2. Enter GM area stage right
3. Position yourself front-center or slightly off-center
4. Stay put if you love the next act too

**Hospitality tent strategy:**
1. Use between sets to cool down/refuel
2. Grab complimentary snacks before peak lunch rush (11:30 AM-12:30 PM)
3. Private restrooms = shorter lines than general fest
4. Good meeting point if group splits up

**Re-entry hack:**
- If you need a proper meal off-site, you can leave and come back
- Walk to nearby restaurants (Parkway Tavern, Toups Meatery), return refreshed
- Just don't drive out if you parked on-site

### Hidden Gems to Use GM Access For

**These acts are PERFECT for front-stage GM access:**
- Thursday: Big Chief Donald Harrison (Congo Square 2:10 PM) - immersive experience
- Friday: GIVERS (Festival Stage 2:10 PM), Seratones (Festival Stage 3:40 PM)
- Saturday: Samantha Fish (Festival Stage 1:55 PM), The Revivalists (Festival Stage 3:35 PM)
- Sunday: River Eckert (Festival Stage 11:15 AM - empty fairgrounds, walk right up)

**When GM access is LEAST valuable:**
- Small tents (Gospel, Economy Hall, Blues) - everyone gets close anyway
- Early morning sets (11-12 PM) - fairgrounds are empty
- Acts with small crowds - you can get close with GA ticket

**When GM access is MOST valuable:**
- Evening headliners (5-7 PM) - massive crowds
- Popular local acts (Preservation Hall, Dirty Dozen, Kermit Ruffins)
- Saturday/Sunday (bigger weekend crowds)
- Rain delays (everyone huddles at covered stages)

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I share my pass with someone during the day?**  
A: No. Whoever gets the wristband that morning has the pass for the entire day.

**Q: What if my wristband breaks?**  
A: Broken wristbands are NOT accepted. Wear it securely and avoid snagging it.

**Q: Can I bring my kids into GM areas?**  
A: Kids 2+ need their own pass and wristband. Under 2 need wristband only (free from gate staff).

**Q: Is the hospitality tent food enough for lunch?**  
A: It's light snacks (think: chips, crackers, fruit). You'll still want festival food for meals.

**Q: Can I use GM access at smaller tents?**  
A: No. GM access is ONLY for Festival Stage, Shell Gentilly Stage, and Congo Square Stage.

**Q: Do I need to show my wristband at the hospitality tent?**  
A: Yes, both laminated pass + wristband required for all GM areas.

**Q: What if I lose my laminated pass during the fest?**  
A: Contact VIP Ticket Operations immediately. Keep it in a secure pocket/bag.
